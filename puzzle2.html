Year Two

The year of firsts.

Fill in the story as it happened — take your time, I've left you hints.

Year two was the year of firsts. We explored through ______ that seemed to stretch on forever.

Think tall, golden, and ... popcorn.

That summer we rode ______ and spent the whole day out on the water at River Rats.

Family, not a stranger's.

Then came November, and matric dance, and I don't think I've ever seen you look more ______ than you did that night.

There's only one right answer here, and you know it!

After Christmas that year, we wandered around ______ to see all the lights.

A shopping place near Sandton... two words.

Somewhere between the lights and the cold, we snuck in more ______ than either of us could count.

Somewhere between a hug and a real kiss.

And that year, we did something exciting. Our first international trip together, all the way to ______.

Stars and stripes.

We also tried ______ for the very first time, and had purple slushies, and took pictures more than we skated.

Cold, slippery, and we were pretty good at it.

And somewhere in the middle of it all, completely out of nowhere, we made a very unexpected little friend, a ______.

It quacked.
